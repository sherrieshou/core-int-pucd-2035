In an Artist's Studio

Christina Rossetti

“A queen in opal or in ruby dress, A nameless girl in freshest summer-greens, A saint, an angel — every canvas means”

Not as she is, but was when hope shone bright; Not as she is, but as she fills his dream.

One face looks out from all his canvases, One selfsame figure sits or walks or leans:

We found her hidden just behind those screens, That mirror gave back all her loveliness.

The same one meaning, neither more or less. He feeds upon her face by day and night, And she with true kind eyes looks back on him, Fair as the moon and joyful as the light: Not wan with waiting, not with sorrow dim;
