What I'm Up To

Drawing these tiny illustrations is one of my hobbby. I see it as a social Tool that I will normally give my drawings as gift to my online friends. Here is a video of a process while I use watercolor.
I am obsessed with the texture of watercolor, and at the same time, I also feel that painting watercolor is very relaxing. In this semester, I completed one or two small illustrations per week on average.
In addition to watercolor, I also use my iPad and oil pastel to draw illustrations. At the same time, I will publish some of the works on video or photo websites, such as Bilibili, TickTock, and Lofter. The maximum number of views of my single video is close to 30,000.
